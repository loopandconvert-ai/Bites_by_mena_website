The story behind the cookies

Hi, I'm Mena 👋

The girl behind Bites by Mena — baking from the heart, one cookie at a time.

👩‍🍳
Where it all began

A kitchen, a dream, and a whole lot of butter

It all started on a quiet weekend when I decided to try baking cookies from scratch. No fancy equipment, no professional kitchen — just me, my oven, and a recipe that had been passed around by family for years.

The cookies came out perfectly golden and the whole house smelled incredible. My friends and family couldn't stop reaching for more, and that's when I thought: "Maybe this is something special."

🍪
The name

Why "Bites by Mena"?

Every cookie is made up of little bits — bits of chocolate, bits of love, bits of time and care. And every bite carries a little piece of me in it. That's how Bites by Mena got its name.

It's not just a product — it's a little handmade gift from my kitchen to yours. I put my heart into every single batch, and I hope you can taste that.

📦
Today

Sharing the love, one order at a time

What started as a fun hobby has grown into a small business that I'm incredibly proud of. Every order is still personally baked and packaged by me — with the same love and care as the very first batch.

Right now I have one signature cookie, my Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie. But this is just the beginning — there are more flavours in the works, and I can't wait to share them with you!
