Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

How do I place an order?

Head over to the Order page, fill in your details and how many cookies you'd like. Mena will confirm your order by email within 24 hours.

How far in advance should I order?

Please order at least 2–3 days in advance to ensure your cookies are baked fresh and delivered on time. For large orders (30+), contact me first.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! Both delivery and pickup are available. You can choose your preference when placing your order.

Do you accommodate allergies?

My cookies contain gluten, dairy, and eggs. If you have a specific allergy or dietary requirement, please mention it in your order notes or contact me directly so we can discuss options.

Can I order cookies for an event or as a gift?

Absolutely! Cookies make the perfect gift. Reach out via the contact form above and we'll work something out together.
